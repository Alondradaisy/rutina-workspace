WHAT IS RUTINA?

RUTINA is an online workspace aiming to enhance the creative and productive workflow in day-to-day life.

Choose a template or create your own page.

  • Take quick notes
  • Make a playlist
  • Upload photos

Our name is derived from the Spanish origin of the English word routine. Our concept comes from the exact definition of the word itself: a sequence of actions regularly followed.

We understand that organization and planning are at the root of success, and we're here to encourage you to build a routine out of it.

Our interactive productive workspace is available via web and iOS app store
